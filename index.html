My Hobbies

Hiking and Climbing

Since I come from a country, that has a landscape full of mountains, I am naturally attracted to hiking and climbing. As a little kid I use to climb up every trea in the neighbourhood. My favourite places to hike are my home country of Slovenia and Italy.

Recently I also started to try out indoor rock climbing. I only do it in the winter months, because of the bad weather outside. I can only advise this amazing sport to everyone. There is something special in defeating a wall by climbing it.

Meditation and Yoga

One of the things that gives me peace of mind in hard times, is definetly spirituality. I really adore buddhism and everything that hast to do with buddhism. So for example, reading buddhist books, listening to buddhist talks, from time to time I even visit a group meditation in a buddhist center.

A lot of my free time is spend on finding always new ways to meditate. To complete spiritual part I do also Yoga. I am still a beginner, but we are getting there.

Writing Poetry

When I was younger, I use to write a lot of poems. It was always rewarding and it allowed me to express my emotions in difficult times. The fun part about it is that everyone can be a poet. My favourite types of poems are Verses. Haiku and Sonnets.

My best sources of inspiration are people, who I care about, the forest, the seaside, and thinking about the past and the future. I also use to own a poetry journal. Sadley it got lost over the years. This is a hobby, that I am planing to bring back to life very soon.

Vegan Cooking

When I first became Vegan, I was suddenly put in a situation, where I had to start cooking for myself. Something that I used to hate, became one of my favourite hobbies. Learning to cook has also saved me a lot of money and time. The meals made at home, are meals made with love, they also taste fresher than for example frozen microwave foods.

I also enjoy to have control over, what I put into my body. Over time, as my cooking skills deeloped, I also started to experiment with different recipes and turning non-vegan dishes into vegan.
